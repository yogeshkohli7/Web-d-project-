MAX

Max, a lovable Labrador, was rescued through Furry Care, where he found his forever home. After being cared for and trained at our facility, he was adopted by a loving family who welcomed him with open arms. Max now enjoys a happy, fulfilled life, showing how Furry Care changes lives through adoption.

Meet Snowy - A Tale of Love and Second Chances

Snowy, a sweet white puppy, was once abandoned and lonely, waiting for a loving family to call her own. When Snowy came to Furry Care, her bright eyes and gentle nature captured everyone's heart. After spending weeks in foster care, Snowy found her forever home with a kind family who instantly fell in love with her playful spirit.
